ABOUT POR EXPRESS

Ten years of moving Cambodia's businesses forward

Founded in 2020, Por Express has grown from a single depot in Phnom Penh into one of Cambodia's most trusted freight and supply chain partners, serving manufacturers, retailers, and exporters across the country.

We combine a modern fleet, purpose-built warehousing, and real-time visibility technology with a team that understands the realities of moving goods across Cambodia's provinces — from congested city routes to rural last-mile delivery.

  • Licensed, insured, and compliant nationwide freight operations
  • 15 strategically located warehouses across major provinces
  • Dedicated account teams for SME and enterprise clients
